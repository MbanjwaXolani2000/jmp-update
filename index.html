Our services

Residential Security

We understand that your home is your sanctuary, and ensuring its safety is our top priority. Our Residential Security Services are tailored to protect your family, property, and peace of mind.

The service includes:

  1. 24/7 On-Site Guards: Highly trained security personnel stationed at your property to monitor and respond promptly to any potential threats.
  2. Mobile Patrols: Regularly scheduled patrols to deter criminal activity and ensure the security of your premises.
  3. Access Control: Professional management of entry points.
Commercial Security

We recognize that protecting your business is essential to maintaining its success and reputation. Our Commercial Security Services are designed to safeguard your assets, employees.

The services includes:

  1. 24/7 On-Site Guards: Highly trained security personnel stationed at your property to monitor and respond promptly to any potential threats.
  2. CCTV Monitoring and Surveillance: Real-time monitoring using advanced surveillance systems intergrated with Artificial Intelligence.
  3. Risk Assessment: Comprehensive analysis and reporting of security incidents to improve preventive measures.
Bodyguard

We specialize in providing professional bodyguarding services to ensure your personal safety and peace of mind. Our experienced and highly trained security personnel are dedicated to protecting you, your loved ones, or your VIP guests in any setting.

The service includes:

  1. Personal Protection: Discreet and vigilant bodyguards to ensure your safety during public appearances, private events, or daily activities.
  2. Risk Assessment and Planning: Comprehensive evaluation of potential threats and development of customized security strategies.
  3. Secure Transportation: Escort services and secure vehicle arrangements to ensure safe travel to and from destinations.
